About Us

 BlueBerry

Blueberry is a general-purpose controller interfaces used to connect computers to electronic devices, sensors, motors, leds& more!

  What we do?

We are a group of professionals, hobbyist and enthusiasts who love to innovate new things.We love coming up with new designs for electronics and spend hours creating gadgets and tools.

 Why choose us?

We love 3D printing and we work hard to bridge the gap between static 3D printed objects and electronic control to allow people a very easy route to create far more dynamic creations.

 Where are we?

We aim to fully productise the BlueBerry Boards and provide a comprehensive online library of software a 3D printable accessories. The website will have a forum where budding creators can discuss their ideas with experienced engineers to make their ideas real.
